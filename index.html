You are eligible to vote if you are:

  • a registered voter
  • at least 18 years of age
  • a US citizen
  • in line at your poll site before it is scheduled to close
  • on probation or parole

At your poll site, you have the right to:

  • vote free from discrimination (Voting Rights Act)
  • assistance for language access or disability-related needs
  • a provisional ballot if your name is missing from the voter list
  • vote without intimidation or harassment
  • report violations and request help
  • not show ID if you are not a first-time voter
